Largest Hockey Arena

The Bell Centre (French: Centre Bell), formerly known as the Molson Centre (or Le Centre Molson), is a multi-purpose arena in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. It opened on March 16, 1996, after nearly three years under construction. It most notable as the home arena for the National Hockey League's Montreal Canadiens. The Bell Centre is the largest hockey arena in the world.
